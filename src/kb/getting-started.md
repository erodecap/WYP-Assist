# Getting Started with WYP Assist

WYP Assist is a suite of entertainment rigging calculation tools designed for riggers, production managers, and technical directors.

## Tools Overview

### Point Load Estimator (Free)
Calculate individual point loads for overhead rigging systems. Enter your total weight, span, and number of points to get load distributions.

### Pull Sheet Generator (Pro)
Generate complete rigging hardware lists. Automatically calculates shackle, sling, hoist, and steel quantities based on your rigging points.

### Bridle Calculator (Pro)
Compute bridle leg lengths, angles, and tensions. Ensures your bridle configurations stay within safe operating limits.

### Markout Generator (Pro)
Create scaled stage plots from CSV data. Import rigging point positions and export professional markout sheets for load-in.

## Workflow

A typical workflow connects the tools together:

1. **Plan** — Use the Point Load Estimator to calculate your point loads
2. **Gear** — Push results to the Pull Sheet to generate your hardware list
3. **Plot** — Import your CSV into the Markout Generator for the stage plot
4. **Verify** — Use the Bridle Calculator for any bridled points
5. **Export** — Generate PDFs for your crew

## Units

All tools support both **Imperial** (lbs, ft) and **Metric** (kg, m) units. Toggle between them at any time.

## Safety Notice

All calculations are estimates for planning purposes only. Always verify with a qualified structural engineer. Minimum safety factor of 5:1 for overhead rigging. Comply with ANSI E1 standards and local codes.
